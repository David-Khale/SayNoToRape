Are You a Rape Victim?

Out of all the girls that get raped, only a few ever tell anyone about their experiances.This doesnt have to be the case for you. At #SNTR, we have multiple psychologists, councellors and people trained to handle these issues. We'll even help you get justice while we're at it. just give us a call and we'll be there for you. Want us to come to you? Report your case with your location and we'll be there ASAp.

Founder of the Stand to End Rape NGO, Adeyoji is an award winning activist who has been nationally recognised for her work in opposing gender violence and rape.

Itoro Eze Anaba

The founder of Mirabel centre, the first ever rape referral centre in Nigeria continues to lead the fight against rape round the nation after drafting the first law agianst dometsic violence.

Read more

Funke Ijayekunle

Funke is the founder of Heartminder's Societal Advancement Initiative, a rape referral agency. A professional therapist, Funke has helped provided emotional support to lots of rape victims.

Read more

Kemi DaSilva Ibru

Kemi formed Women Against Rape International Foundation (WARIF) in response to the high amount of rape cases in Nigeria. She has organised protests, helped rape victims, and continues to lead the fight against rape.
